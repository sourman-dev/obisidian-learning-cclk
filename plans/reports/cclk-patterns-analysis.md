## 1. Core Philosophy: Luc Khi vs Ngu Hanh

### Key Difference
| Aspect | Ngu Hanh (Traditional) | Luc Khi (CCLK) |
|--------|------------------------|----------------|
| Elements | 5 (Moc, Hoa, Tho, Kim, Thuy) | 6 (+Thu/Summer-heat) |
| Origin | Yin from Bat Quai | Yang from Bat Quai |
| Focus | External correspondences | Internal Qi transformations of Zang-Fu |

### The 6 Elements with Correspondences

| Luc Khi | Meaning | Element | Meridian Type |
|---------|---------|---------|---------------|
| Han khi | Cold | **Thuy** | Thai Duong |
| Tao khi | Dry | **Kim** | Duong Minh |
| Hoa khi | Hot | **Hoa** | Thieu Duong |
| Thu khi | Warm | **Thu** | Thieu Am |
| Phong khi | Wind | **Moc** | Quyet Am |
| Thap khi | Damp | **Tho** | Thai Am |

### Luc Khi Cycles (MEMORIZE THIS ORDER)

**Tuong Sinh (Generation):**
```
Tho -> Kim -> Thuy -> Thu -> Moc -> Hoa -> Tho
```

**Tuong Khac (Restriction) - 3 Pairs:**
- Thuy - Hoa (Water-Fire)
- Tho - Thu (Earth-Summer-heat)
- Kim - Moc (Metal-Wood)

## 2. Ngu Du Huyet (5 Transport Points) Rules

### Point Order (Distal to Proximal)

| Position | Point Type | Kinh Am (Yin) | Kinh Duong (Yang) |
|----------|------------|---------------|-------------------|
| 1 | Tinh (Well) | Yes | Yes |
| 2 | Vinh (Spring) | Yes | Yes |
| 3 | Du (Stream) | Yes | Yes |
| 4 | Nguyen (Source) | **NO** | Yes |
| 5 | Kinh (River) | Yes (pos 4) | Yes |
| 6 | Hop (Sea) | Yes (pos 5) | Yes |

**KEY RULE:** Yang meridians have 6 points (extra Nguyen), Yin meridians have 5 points.

### Location Pattern Rules

| Point | Location Pattern |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Tinh** | Corner of nail, ~0.1 cun from nail |
| **Vinh** | Between metacarpo/metatarso-phalangeal joints |
| **Du** | Near wrist/ankle joints |
| **Nguyen** | On wrist/ankle (Yang only) |
| **Kinh** | On forearm/leg, 1+ cun from wrist/ankle |
| **Hop** | Near elbow/knee joints |

## 3. Element Assignment Rules (The Core Pattern)

### Step 1: Determine HANH (Intrinsic Element) of Organ

| HANH | Tho | Kim | Thuy | Thu | Moc | Hoa |
|------|-----|-----|------|-----|-----|-----|
| **Tang (Yin)** | Ty | Phe | Than | Tam | Can | TBL |
| **Phu (Yang)** | Vi | Dai Truong | Bang Quang | Tieu Truong | Dom | Tam Tieu |

### Step 2: Determine BO (Set Position)

**RULE FOR YIN:** BO = HANH (same element)

**RULE FOR YANG:** BO = element that GENERATES the organ's HANH
- Use: `Tho -> Kim -> Thuy -> Thu -> Moc -> Hoa -> Tho`

| Yang Organ | HANH | What generates HANH? | BO |
|------------|------|---------------------|-----|
| Dai Truong | Kim | Tho generates Kim | **Tho** |
| Bang Quang | Thuy | Kim generates Thuy | **Kim** |
| Tieu Truong | Thu | Thuy generates Thu | **Thuy** |
| Dom | Moc | Thu generates Moc | **Thu** |
| Tam Tieu | Hoa | Moc generates Hoa | **Moc** |
| Vi | Tho | Hoa generates Tho | **Hoa** |

### Step 3: Assign Element to Each Point

**RULE FOR TINH (Point 1):** Element = what the BO generates
- Tinh HANH = BO's child in Tuong Sinh cycle

**RULE FOR SUBSEQUENT POINTS:** Follow Tuong Sinh cycle from Tinh element

**SPECIAL RULE - DU (Point 3):** Often RESTRICTS the meridian's BO
- Uses Tuong Khac pairs

**SPECIAL RULE - HOP (Point 5/6 for Yang):** Matches the meridian's BO

| Aspect | Kinh Am (Yin) | Kinh Duong (Yang) |

## 6. Treatment Principles

### Core Concept: HOA (Harmony)
- Goal: Restore balance between organs
- Principle: "Binh Thong Nhau" (Communicating Vessels)
- Energy flows from excess to deficiency

### 4 Acupuncture Approaches

| Approach | Meridians Used | Total Points |
|----------|---------------|--------------|
| Thu Cham (Hand) | 6 hand meridians | 13 |
| Tuc Cham (Foot) | 6 foot meridians | 13 |
| Am Cham (Yin) | 6 Yin meridians | 14 |
| Duong Cham (Yang) | 6 Yang meridians | 12 |

### Building a Point Set (Bo Huyet)

1. **Select Chu Kinh (Main):** Meridian whose BO matches treatment goal
2. **Eliminate 1:** Remove meridian whose BO restricts Chu Kinh's BO
3. **Connect 8 points:** 4 from Chu Kinh (skip Du) + 4 from Phu Kinh
4. **Add 4 Du points:** 2 pairs from restricting BOs
5. **Conditional:** Add Yang Hop if Yin Tinh present

### Connection Rule
```
Point of element 'a' on Meridian B <-> Point of element 'b' on Meridian A
```

## 7. Flashcard-Ready Patterns

### Quick Memorization: Tuong Sinh Cycle
```
"Tho Kim Thuy Thu Moc Hoa" (Earth Metal Water Summer Wood Fire)
```

### Quick Memorization: Tuong Khac Pairs
```
"Thuy-Hoa, Tho-Thu, Kim-Moc" (3 pairs of opposites)
```

### Pattern for Yang BO
```
Yang BO = "Mother" of HANH in generation cycle
```

### Pattern for Tinh Element
```
Tinh = "Child" of BO in generation cycle
```

### Pattern for Hop Element (Yang only)
```
Hop = BO (always matches)
```

### Pattern for Du Element
```
Du often restricts BO (check Tuong Khac pairs)
```
